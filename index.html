About

I architect highly available, security-hardened microservice ecosystems that survive contact with real production traffic — and I build the engineering cultures that keep shipping them fast. Across enterprise SaaS, FinTech, and employee-benefits platforms, my focus has consistently been the intersection of system design rigor and delivery velocity: decomposing brittle monoliths into resilient, independently deployable services, and instrumenting API contracts that scale partner onboarding instead of bottlenecking it.

In the last few years I've pushed further into AI-native engineering — designing custom prompt and context-engineering libraries and autonomous-agent frameworks that have driven measurable, board-reportable outcomes across multiple teams, not just my own backlog.

I lead from the architecture diagram and the pull request — owning technical roadmaps and executive conversations while staying hands-on enough to mentor engineers through advanced design patterns and secure coding practice. Several of my mentees have since moved into senior roles themselves.
